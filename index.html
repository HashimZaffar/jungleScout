Account Setup

Follow these steps to create and manage your Jungle Scout account.

Step 1: Creating an Account

To get started with Jungle Scout, visit the Jungle Scout website and click on the "Sign Up" button.

Fill in your email, password, and other required details, then click "Create Account". You will receive a confirmation email. Click on the link in the email to verify your account.

Step 2: Choosing a Subscription Plan

After verifying your account, you will be prompted to choose a subscription plan. Jungle Scout offers various plans depending on your needs. Select the plan that best suits your requirements.

Step 3: Setting Up Your Profile

Once your subscription is active, you can set up your profile. Navigate to the profile settings page, where you can add your name, contact information, and preferences.

Step 4: Payment Information

To manage your subscription and billing, enter your payment information in the billing section. You can update this information at any time.

Step 5: Accessing the Dashboard

After completing your account setup, you will be directed to the Jungle Scout dashboard. Here you can start using the tools to research products, optimize listings, and more.

Product Research

Learn how to use Jungle Scout's tools to find profitable products to sell on Amazon.

Step 1: Accessing the Product Database

To begin your product research, go to the Jungle Scout dashboard and select "Product Database" from the main menu. This tool allows you to search for products based on various criteria like category, price, and sales.

Step 2: Setting Filters

Use filters to narrow down your search. You can filter by product category, sales, price range, review count, and more. This helps you find products that meet your specific criteria.

Step 3: Analyzing Product Data

Once you've applied your filters, you'll see a list of products that match your criteria. Analyze the data by looking at metrics like sales volume, revenue, review count, and ratings. These metrics help you assess the profitability and competition of each product.

Step 4: Adding Products to the Product Tracker

If you find a product that looks promising, you can add it to the "Product Tracker" to monitor its performance over time. This tool allows you to track sales, inventory levels, and price changes, giving you a better understanding of the product's market potential.

Step 5: Saving Searches and Exporting Data

Save your search filters and export the data for further analysis. This feature is useful for keeping track of multiple product ideas and sharing them with your team.
